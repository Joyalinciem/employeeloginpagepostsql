Core Capabilities

Everything your team needs, secured by design

From creating tasks to publishing reports, every action in TaskFlow is governed by role permissions and protected by multi-layer security.

Role-Based Access

Admin, CTO, Manager, and User roles each carry specific granular permissions. Create tasks, delete records, or approve users — all based on your assigned role.

Multi-Factor Auth

Protect every login with Email OTP (6-digit codes via SMTP) or TOTP via Google Authenticator. 2FA is configurable per user from the profile dashboard.

Kanban Boards

Visualize work across Open, In Progress, and Completed stages. Move tasks forward, set priorities, and track who's responsible for what.

Org Hierarchy

Model your company structure. Each user can be assigned a Manager and CTO. Tasks inherit hierarchy visibility so leaders see what their teams are working on.

Audit & Logging

Every login, role change, task update, and approval is logged chronologically. API-level structured logs are auto-archived daily for compliance.

In-App Notifications

Users are instantly notified when they're assigned a manager, promoted, or when their tasks are updated. No missed context, no manual follow-ups.

Role Permissions

Every role, exactly what they need

TaskFlow's RBAC engine gives you fine-grained control over what each team member can create, read, update, or delete. Toggle roles below to preview live permission limits.

✓ Admins have full platform control including user approvals
✓ CTOs can manage tasks and view all team activity

Security Layer

Two-factor protection, your way

TaskFlow enforces multi-factor authentication at the application layer. Users can choose between email OTP or authenticator app TOTP — managed entirely from their profile.

Email OTP
A 6-digit code is sent to the user's registered email via SMTP. Valid for 5 minutes. No additional app needed.
TOTP (Google Authenticator)
Scan a QR code and sync your account to any TOTP app. Time-based codes rotate every 30 seconds — zero reliance on email.
